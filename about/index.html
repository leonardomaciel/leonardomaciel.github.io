My name is Leonardo Maciel Delgado. I'm a multifunctional designer with over 15 years of experience in creating digital products

<

I've been creating interfaces and products since 2004, always working with the user experience for digital products and services.

I have developed various types of knowledge in my career. I started as a Web Designer at a publishing house and had a stint as a developer which helps me a lot nowadays. In 2010 I opened Coffeecup, a digital projects producer and in 2015 we became FERA, a digital products agency and I had the experience of leading the product design team for large companies such as Bluefit, GarageInn, Instituto Ayrton Senna, Porto Seguro, among others.

In 2015 at Fera, we focused on developing digital products for various clients and was responsible for the UX team acting as Lead Designer.
